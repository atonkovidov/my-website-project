Historical Town Tour

Courtesy of the official Historical Society, Toluca Lake offers very inexpensive and educational daily walking tours of our fair lakeside town. Starting at the centuries-old Town Hall, winding its way through long streets (careful to not get seperated when it's foggy!), and eventually ending up at the recently-restored docks, the tour leaves no part of town untouched. Before taking the tour, why not have a look at the town hall itself?
